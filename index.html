Every empty shelf is a market-share decision made without the brand in the room.

A kid drags his mom to the freezer for his favorite ice cream. The slot's empty, so they grab the competitor — or nothing at all. The sale is gone. The distributor doesn't know. The rep won't find out until next week — if ever. Multiply that by 50 million stores, 365 days a year.

The intelligence layer

Dynamic business rules,
in real time — for the first time.

Business rules are a brand's IP — the proprietary logic behind how they decide at the shelf, built from years of hard-earned market knowledge. Wisy runs that logic against the shelf as it actually is, right now, and routes the rep to the highest-value fix before they leave.

The result

Rules that get smarter.

Wisy doesn't just validate the rules against the shelf — it finds what they're missing. AI evaluates detections across every store, surfaces patterns, and recommends changes that drive revenue.
